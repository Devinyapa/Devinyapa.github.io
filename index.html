Sobawitha 2022

Sobawitha is a web application designed to streamline and modernize fertilizer management. It offers users a user-friendly platform to search for, purchase, and rate fertilizers, as well as providing valuable feedback. For sellers, it facilitates procurement, access to instructions, and efficient product promotion. The app also fosters communication among agri-officers, farmers, producers, and suppliers, enhancing collaboration in the industry. This project allowed me to develop expertise in web development, database management, and software engineering, while also strengthening my project management and teamwork skills. It represents my commitment to leveraging technology for positive change in the agriculture sector.
